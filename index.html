    About me

    As a Communications Assistant at Egypt’s Ministry of International Cooperation, I have worked on setting up the Ministry’s new interactive website to help audiences track and visualize Egypt’s development through a map and improve transparency and access to information. On top of that, I have also worked on publishing reports and articles, writing social media captions for the Ministry’s page and the Minister’s page, as well as producing video content that focuses on grassroots storytelling. It is worth mentioning that Minister Rania A. Al-Mashat was the first ever Egyptian Minister to have an Instagram account in 2018, and later in 2021 a Linkedin blog which I helped launch.
